Description:
Abstract:
The roads layer was digitized from USDMA 1:100,000 scale. The layer includes 3 type of roads, all weather primary, all weather secondary and tracks.
Purpose:
Road layers provide basemap data on road and track features at a 1:100,000 scale. Users must assume responsibility in determining the usability of this data for their purpose. Not for use in scales greater than 1:100,000.
